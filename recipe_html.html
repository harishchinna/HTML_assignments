Maggi Recipe

Ingradients

  • maggi
  • oil
  • Masala
  • Onions
  • vegetables
  • Veniger
  • Sos
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • cheese

Process

  1. Boil 1 1/2 cups water in a pan and add Maggi Noodles and Tastemaker.
  2. Add some quantity of oil to it.
  3. Simmer for two minutes till the Maggi is fully cooked.
  4. Meanwhile, beat an egg till frothy. Add the egg to the Maggi once the maggi is cooked
  5. Add Onion slices
  6. Add some sliced vegetables like tomatos,coriander etc..
  7. Add sos,veniger to give better taste.
  8. Top with cheese and mix. Serve immediately.
